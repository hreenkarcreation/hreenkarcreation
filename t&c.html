Return & Exchange Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Policy Overview

At Hreenkar Creation and Kambeshwar Agencies, we are committed to providing quality products to our retail partners. This Return Policy outlines the conditions and procedures for returning or exchanging products purchased from us.

Important: This policy applies to business-to-business (B2B) transactions with our retail partners. End consumers should follow the brand's consumer return policy through their point of purchase.

2. Eligibility for Returns

2.1 Acceptable Return Reasons

Returns will be accepted in the following cases:

  • Manufacturing Defects: Products with clear manufacturing defects identified upon delivery
  • Damaged Products: Items damaged during transit (must be reported immediately)
  • Wrong Product Delivered: Incorrect items or sizes shipped
  • Missing Items: Shortfall in delivered quantity vs. invoiced quantity

2.2 Non-Returnable Items

The following items are NOT eligible for return:

  • Products damaged after delivery or due to improper storage
  • Items with removed or tampered tags, labels, or packaging
  • Products used, worn, washed, or altered
  • Innerwear opened from sealed packaging (hygiene reasons)
  • Special order or customized items
  • Products purchased on clearance or final sale
  • Returns beyond the specified return window

3. Return Timeline

3.1 Reporting Window

  • Delivery Issues: Must be reported within 24 hours of delivery
  • Manufacturing Defects: Must be reported within 7 days of delivery
  • Wrong Products: Must be reported within 48 hours of delivery

3.2 Return Completion

Products must be returned within 15 days from the date of issue reporting and approval.

4. Return Process

Step 1: Report the Issue

Contact us immediately via:

  • Phone: +91 94225 93814 | +91 88880 99776
  • Email: hreenkarcreation@gmail.com
  • Provide: Invoice number, product details, issue description, and photos (if applicable)

Step 2: Return Authorization

  • Our team will verify your claim and invoice details
  • Do not return products without prior authorization

Step 3: Product Return

  • Pack products securely in original packaging (if available)
  • Attach copy of original invoice
  • Return products to our address or arrange pickup (as per agreement)

Step 4: Inspection and Resolution

  • Returned products will be inspected within 5-7 business days
  • You will be notified of the inspection outcome
  • Approved returns will be processed as per resolution option

5. Return Resolution Options

5.1 Replacement

  • Defective/wrong products will be replaced with correct items
  • Replacement shipment subject to stock availability
  • No additional charges for replacement shipping

5.2 Credit Note

  • Credit note issued for value of returned products
  • Can be adjusted against future purchases
  • Valid for 6 months from issue date

6. Shipping and Return Costs

6.1 Our Responsibility

We bear return shipping costs for:

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Wrong products delivered by us
  • Damaged products during transit (with proof)

6.2 Buyer's Responsibility

Buyer bears return shipping costs for:

  • Size/style exchanges

7. Special Conditions

7.1 Bulk Orders

  • Special return terms may apply for large bulk orders
  • Must be discussed and agreed upon during order placement
  • Documented in purchase order or agreement

7.2 Seasonal/Promotional Items

  • May have modified return windows
  • Clearance items generally not eligible for return
  • Special terms communicated at time of purchase

8. Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes regarding returns:

  • We will make every effort to resolve issues amicably
  • Product inspection reports may be used for final decision
  • Management decision will be final in ambiguous cases

Important Notes:

  • Always inspect products upon delivery before signing delivery receipt
  • Note any visible damage or discrepancies on delivery document
  • Keep original packaging and invoice for all return scenarios
  • Photograph issues for faster claim processing
